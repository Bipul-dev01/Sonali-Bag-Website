Eco Friendly

Sonali Bag revolutionizes sustainability by incentivizing farmers to cultivate jute, fostering a natural ecosystem and biodiversity. Harnessing the remarkable carbon-absorbing properties of jute plants, Sonali Bag reduces greenhouse gas emissions by continuing a natural ecosystem and biodiversity system. The production process is also a carbon-negative, eco-friendly marvel, contributing significantly to environmental preservation." By commercializing Sonali Bag, we contribute to reducing greenhouse gas emissions, fostering a carbon-negative and eco-friendly solution—an extraordinary achievement for a greener future.

Biodegradable and compostable

Sonali Bag, a groundbreaking eco-friendly solution, undergoes complete degradation within 3-4 months through the action of soil-dwelling natural microorganisms like Trichoderma fungi and Acetobacter xyleium bacteria, cellulose enzyme, etc. Following its eco-friendly decomposition, Sonali Bag transforms into a nutrient-rich bio-fertilizer. Enriched with nitrogen components, this bio- fertilizer not only facilitates sustainable waste management but also significantly reduces farmers' cultivation costs, fostering a harmonious and cost-effective approach to crop cultivation.

Affordable

"Sonali Bag, a versatile alternative to plastic, boasts convenience with its lightweight design, making it easy to carry for various applications. Developed in response to consumer demands, Sonali Bag offers features such as water solubility, water resistance, and adaptability to carry products ranging from low to high weight. Our dedicated research and development team is committed to addressing and solving the diverse requirements of consumers.
