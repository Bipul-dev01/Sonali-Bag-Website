Significance of Sonali Bag

✓ Sonali Bag is a biodegradable biopolymer, made of jute cellulose

✓ Totally biodegradable, compostable, water soluble, recyclable, and sustainable.

✓ Does not melt in fire like plastic materials, it produces ash during incineration.

✓ Sonali Bag can be customized as per requirement of consumer like as water soluble, water resistant, etc.

✓ Tensile strength is 1.6 more times than polyethylene.

✓ Raw materials are renewable, and abundant.

✓ Production process is simple and environment friendly.

Other potential use of Jute-based Biopolymer

Jute-based biopolymer has significant environmental and product benefits. The biopolymer can be used in the following industries and sectors :

✓ Product Packaging: Jute base polymeric sheet can be used in food-grade and non-food grade packaging for various types of products.

✓ Sanitary Products: This polymer sheet use in diapers and sanitary napkin as back part.

✓ Agricultural Sector: Biopolymer has already been tested and proven to be very effective and alternate solution for agriculture sector, particularly for seedling, mulching, etc.

✓ Medical Sector: It can be used as personal protective equipment (Gowon, mask, head cap, hand gloves), one time bed sheet.

Sonali Bag®

The Sonali bag is an eco-friendly and sustainable alternative to the current polyethylene and polypropylene bags – one of the major causes of plastic pollution in the world. Biodegradable, eco-friendly, recyclable, mixed up with the soil within 3-4 months of its disposal and 1.6 times stronger than polyethylene, the bag is made from jute cellulose, a major crop in Bangladesh.
